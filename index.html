About

SHEHNAZDEEP

Phd Scholar

Ms. Shehnazdeep is a PhD student in the Department of Civil Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati, India. She received her Master’s degree in Structural and Construction Engineering from Dr. B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology (NIT), Jalandhar in 2019, where concrete technology captured her interest. Her passion for research and curiosity to learn more about ……..led her to enroll in a Ph.D. program after her master’s. She is a recipient of the prestigious Prime Minister’s Research Fellowship (PMRF), awarded to the country’s excellent doctoral candidates for pursuing their research.


Her research interests include:
  • Geopolymer concrete
  • Concrete durability
  • Construction materials
