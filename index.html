About

SHEHNAZDEEP

PMRS Research Scholar

Ms. Shehnazdeep is a PhD student in the Department of Civil Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati, India. She received her Master’s degree in Structural and Construction Engineering from Dr. B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology (NIT), Jalandhar in 2019, and Bachlor of Engineering (B.E.) in civil engineering from Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology in 2015. She joined IIT Guwahati in Jan 2020 under the supervision of Dr . Bulu Pradhan. She is a recipient of the prestigious Prime Minister's Researsh fellowship (PMRS) awarded to the country's excellent doctoral candidates to pursue Ph.D. in the areas of Civil Engineering.


Her research interests include:
  • Geopolymer concrete
  • Sustainable concrete
  • Durability studies of geopolymer concrete
  • Corrosion monitoring and prevention techniques
